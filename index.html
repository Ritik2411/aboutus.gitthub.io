The history about to do it properly


VESTJYSK AUTHENTICITY AND GOOD CRAFTS


Getting a new kitchen is a big decision. So why not do it properly? This has been our philosophy since the first hinge was fitted over 50 years ago. Fortunately, it has turned out that there are many who share our position.

Our history

It all started in 1968, when our first kitchen was assembled in a joinery somewhere on the harbor in Hanstholm. Although the setting was more modest than it is today, the ambition was the same: To make a tailor-made kitchen according to old craft traditions that the owner will be happy and the carpenter can be proud of..

Vision & Values

Why should a kitchen drawer be 60 inches wide if you fancy one that is 62? It has always been our approach to making kitchens. We are driven by a passion for unique solutions, good craftsmanship and the desire to go the extra mile to ensure that the result is exactly as the customer wants it.

We will be the best at delivering joinery kitchens and modules where the quality is top notch from end to end. Also in the collections inside the closet that you do not notice. Because we do.

Because we are proud of our work. That's why we burn our logo into the side of the drawers. As proof that you are very far from standard kitchens and quick fixes. At the same time, you will also be reminded that you should exercise the same care and respect for the quality of the ingredients when you and your family get loose behind the pots.
